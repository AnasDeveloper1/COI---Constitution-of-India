Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy governs the manner in which the Constitution of India mobile application ("the App") handles user information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a secure user experience.

1. Information Collection and Use

We do not collect, request, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise location. The App is built to run as a reference tool, and you can use all its features without creating an account or providing any personal details.

2. Local Data Storage (Bookmarks & Notes)

The App allows you to save bookmarks and write personal study notes. Please note that all bookmarks and notes are stored strictly locally on your own device's internal storage. We do not have access to your personal notes or bookmarked items, and this data is never uploaded to any external cloud servers.

3. Third-Party Services

We do not partner with or use third-party analytics or behavioral tracking SDKs that monitor your activity. If the App displays standard advertisements or utilizes basic platform services in the future, they will comply with standard Google Play store policies, and no personal profile information is gathered by us.

4. Children's Privacy

The App contains purely educational materials regarding the Constitution, amendments, and schedules of India. It does not address anyone under the age of 13 for malicious data collection purposes, and it is completely safe for students and users of all ages.

5. Security

Since your app data (bookmarks and notes) remains on your physical device, its security depends on your device's security settings. We encourage you to keep your device operating system updated to safeguard your local files.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to align with new legal requirements or app updates. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective immediately when posted on this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions, suggestions, or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please feel free to reach out to us through our developer contact options provided on the application store platform.
